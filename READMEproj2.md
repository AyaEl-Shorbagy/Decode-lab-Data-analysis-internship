# 📊 Sales Analysis Project

## Overview
This project analyzes sales data for e-store using Python to understand customer behavior and business performance.

---

## Objectives
- Analyze sales trends over time  
- Understand order status distribution  
- Explore payment methods usage  
- Identify factors affecting revenue  

---

## Tools Used
- Python  
- Pandas  
- Matplotlib  
- Seaborn  

---

## Key Analysis
- Total sales per year  
- Order status analysis  
- Payment method distribution  
- Relationship between quantity and total price  
- Distribution of sales values  

---

## Key Insights
- Sales show seasonal fluctuations  
- High percentage of cancelled and returned orders  
- Weak relationship between payment method and order status  
- Most orders are low-value  

---

## Business Recommendations
- Focus on increasing average order value  
- Reduce cancelled and returned orders  
- Improve delivery process  
- Optimize marketing strategies during peak periods
